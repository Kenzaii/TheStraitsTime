29-year-old man dies in suspected drowning at East Coast Park

Published: 4th December 2024, 9:17am Updated: 4th December 2024, 9:17am
The incident occurred at East Coast Park on December 4, 2024. PHOTO: YOUR CREDIT SOURCE

A 29-year-old man has died in a suspected drowning incident at East Coast Park. The incident occurred early in the morning, drawing attention from park visitors and emergency services.

According to eyewitnesses, the man was seen struggling in the water before disappearing beneath the surface. Lifeguards and emergency responders were quickly on the scene, but unfortunately, the man could not be revived.

The authorities are currently investigating the circumstances surrounding the incident. This tragic event serves as a reminder of the importance of water safety and the need for vigilance when swimming in open waters.

The police have been contacted for more information.
